About
EagleOCR is a WebApplication for Optical Character Recognition (OCR) with over more than 60 languages support. EagleOCR allows the users to download the text into 3 different formats - .txt(Text File), .docx(MS Word File), .pdf (PDF File). This WebApplication also has the feature of text to voice generator that means you can hear your text, just by clicking on the Speaker button provided at the bottom right corner of your generated text.
Tutorial
► First you need to Click on either the Browse File Button or Camera Button just above the Browse Button. When you click on the Camera Button, your device's camera will get start and then you can simply click on the 'Click' button and your photo will get save. When you click on the Browse button, your device's File Manager will get open and then can select the picture that you want to process.
► After selecting the Image File, you have to select the Language in which the Image file has been written. You can use the search bar for this purpose.
► After completing the first two steps, you will get your generated text the window shown in the image.
► You can easily download your generated text just by clicking on the Download button first and then by clicking on the file format icon. EagleOCR provides you the options for downloading the generated text in .txt, .docx and .pdf formats.
► You can hear the generatd text by our Text to Voice service by just clicking on the speaker icon on the bottom right corner of the generated text window.
FAQs
► Do I have to pay for downloading the generated text?
No, EagleOCR is FREE of cost. You don't have to pay anything to use our services.
► Is my uploaded data safe?
Yes, it is completely safe. Your uploaded data cannot be accessed by anyone else, not even us(EagleOCR).
► Is there any registration or sign up required to use this service?
No, EagleOCR won't ask you for any registration or sign up. But you can give your valuable feedback to us if you want.
► Can we download the generated text?
Yes, you can download your generated text from the image, just by clicking on the download button and then choosing your requried file format.
► Can we use JPEG Format for image?
Yes, In EagleOCR you can upload images of following extensions - .jpg, .jpeg and .png
► Is text to voice service available for Hindi language as well?
Yes, Text to Voice service is available for all the languages.
